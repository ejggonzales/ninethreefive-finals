Different Types of Bags

Elevate your style with our beautifully crafted crochet bags. We have different types, each designed for different needs and styles. Choose from our categories, or you can reach out to us if you wish to have a customized order. Each piece is meticulously handmade, offering both functionality and unique charm to fit your fashion and lifestyle.

Pop-Up Booths

We collaborate with other local businesses wherein we open pop-up booths and sell our products for a number of days. These partnerships have allowed us to strengthen our community ties, and to further reach a wider audience that may want to buy our bags. By working together with other businesses, we strive to bring fresh perspectives and innovative touches to our bags in order to make them more special.

Materials & Accessories

In creating our bags, we use Velvet and Rib-knit yarns. Velvet yarn adds a soft, luxurious touch and a glossy finish that catches the eye while Rib-knit gives a sturdy, stretchy base that holds its shape and can handle daily wear. By using these two as our bag's key material, we are able to create elegant and durable crochet bags.
